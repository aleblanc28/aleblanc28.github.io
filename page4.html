Veronica Ivanovskaia

German pool player. She began to play in 2003, and later became part of the German national team. She was the winner of the 2018 European Pool Championships eight-ball event, and the runner-up at the 2017 Austria Open.

Pia Filler

German professional pool player from Stolberg, Germany. Pia is married to 2018 WPA World Nine-ball Championship winner Joshua Filler. Filler is a German national champion on 11 occasions. Filler has also been successful on the Euro Tour, reaching the semi-final of the 2018 Portugal Open.
